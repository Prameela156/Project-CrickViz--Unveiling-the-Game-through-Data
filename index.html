Bubble chart

This interactive bubble chart provides a comprehensive visualization of IPL batsmen's career statistics, offering insights into their performance based on key metrics. Each bubble in the chart represents an individual batsman, with the following attributes visually encoded:

Y-Position: Represents the batsman's batting average, highlighting consistency across their career.

Radius of the Bubble: Proportional to the total runs scored, emphasizing the overall contribution of each batsman.

Color of the Bubble: Indicates the strike rate, with a gradient scale reflecting the batsman's scoring speed.
